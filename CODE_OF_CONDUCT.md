# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to maintaining a professional and respectful environment in all interactions related to Polaris Focus™ and this repository.

## Expected Behavior

When engaging with this repository or Polaris Focus™:

- Be respectful and professional in all communications
- Respect intellectual property rights, including copyrights and trademarks
- Follow all applicable laws and regulations
- Honor the proprietary nature of Polaris Focus™ and its assets

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- Comments on this repository
- Issue reports or discussions
- Any direct communications with Kaamos Labs related to Polaris Focus™
- Social media interactions concerning Polaris Focus™

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are unacceptable:

### Legal Violations
- Misuse or infringement of trademarks, copyrights, or other intellectual property
- Attempting to reverse engineer or decompile the Polaris Focus™ application
- Unauthorized use of Kaamos Labs' intellectual property

### Communication Standards
- Harassing, discriminatory, or offensive language
- Personal attacks or inflammatory statements
- Spam, trolling, or disruptive behavior
- Sharing false or misleading information

### Technical Abuse
- Attempting to exploit or compromise the Polaris Focus™ application
- Submitting malicious code or content to this repository
- Attempting to deface or modify this repository's contents

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

- Warning notifications
- Temporary or permanent restriction from interacting with this repository
- Reporting to GitHub for platform violations
- Legal action for serious violations of intellectual property or security

## Trademark and Intellectual Property

**Important**: Unauthorized use of the Polaris Focus™ trademark or infringement of Kaamos Labs' intellectual property is strictly prohibited and may result in immediate enforcement action.

Please review [TRADEMARK.md](TRADEMARK.md) and [LICENSE.md](LICENSE.md) for important information about intellectual property rights.

## Reporting

If you encounter unacceptable behavior related to this repository or Polaris Focus™, please contact us through [polarisfocus.com](https://polarisfocus.com).

## Respect and Professionalism

We ask everyone to maintain a professional and respectful tone in all interactions. This helps create a positive environment and protects the integrity of the Polaris Focus™ brand and community.

## Thank You

Thank you for your cooperation and respect for our community standards and intellectual property rights.
