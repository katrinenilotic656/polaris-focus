# Security Policy

## Supported Versions

Security updates are provided for the current release of Polaris Focus™. Please ensure you are using the latest version available through official channels.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take security seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability in Polaris Focus™, please report it responsibly.

### How to Report

1. **DO NOT** create a public GitHub issue for security vulnerabilities
2. Contact us through our website at [polarisfocus.com](https://polarisfocus.com)
3. Include as much detail as possible about:
   - The nature of the vulnerability
   - Steps to reproduce the issue
   - Potential impact or exploit scenarios
   - Any suggested fixes or mitigations

### What to Expect

- **Initial Response**: We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 3 business days
- **Status Updates**: We will provide regular updates on our progress toward resolution
- **Resolution Timeline**: We aim to address critical vulnerabilities within 30 days, depending on complexity
- **Credit**: With your permission, we would be happy to acknowledge your responsible disclosure

### Security Contact

For security-related concerns, please contact us through our support channels at [polarisfocus.com](https://polarisfocus.com). Please clearly indicate in your message that you are reporting a security issue.

### Scope

This security policy applies to:
- The Polaris Focus™ application
- Any associated services or infrastructure operated by Kaamos Labs

Please note: This repository does not contain source code. Security disclosures should focus on issues in the Polaris Focus™ application itself.

### Out of Scope

The following are considered out of scope:
- Social engineering attacks
- Physical security issues
- Denial of service attacks
- Issues in third-party applications or services

### Disclosure Policy

We ask that you:
- Act in good faith to avoid privacy violations, destruction of data, or interruption of service
- Not exploit the vulnerability beyond what is necessary to demonstrate it
- Keep details confidential until we have had time to address the issue

Once a security issue is resolved, we may publicly disclose the vulnerability and acknowledgment in a security advisory, with your permission.

## Thank You

Thank you for helping to keep Polaris Focus™ secure. We appreciate your responsible disclosure and commitment to protecting our users.
